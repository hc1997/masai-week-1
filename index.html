SuperMan

Superman is a fictional character, a superhero appearing in American
comic books published by DC Comics. The character was created by writer
Jerry Siegel and artist Joe Shuster, and first appeared in Action Comics #1.
Although Superman was not the first superhero character, he popularized the
superhero genre and defined its conventions. He remains the best selling superhero
in comic books of all time and endured as one of the most lucrative franchises
even outside of comic books.


Character Traits

Powers:   Super Strength,flight,invulnerability,super speed,heat vision,
              freeze breath,x-ray vision, superhuman hearing,healing factor.

Alias:     Man Of Steel.

Real Name:     Kal-El, Clark Joseph Kent.




Comic Books

Since 1938, Superman stories have been regularly published
in periodical comic books published by DC Comics. The
first and oldest of these is Action Comics, which began
in April 1938.Action Comics was initially an anthology
magazine,but it eventually became dedicated to
Superman stories.The 2nd oldest periodical is Superman
,which began in June 1939. Action Comics and Superman
have been published without interruption

Birth of a Superhero

April 18th,1938

Superman has been depicted as a hero that
originated from the planet Krypton and was
given the name Kal-El at birth. As a baby
, he was sent to Earth in a small spaceship
by his biological family, Jor-El and Lara.
